# Stage 03 - avoided failure modes

- Asset orphan checked: none planned.
- Machine without target checked: none planned.
- Stock without consumption zone checked: none planned.
- Spawn blocked checked: no blocking footprint planned inside 350px.
- Missing path connectivity checked: entry, truck access, stock, secondary prep, and pour zone are connected.
- Excessive density checked: medium density budget, no global clutter.
- Mixer truck alone avoided: trucks are in signature or truck access scenes.
- Concrete pump alone avoided: pump exists only in signature and points to slab/formwork bay.
- Concrete mixer alone avoided: mixer exists only with formwork and spill.
- Rebar scattered randomly avoided: rebar exists only in stock, ready, bay, or signature scenes.
- Formwork scattered randomly avoided: formwork appears inside named bay, ready, patch, or signature scenes.
- Spill far from concrete avoided: every spill is near slab, bay, or patch.
- Too many cracks avoided: one minor edge defect maximum.
- Competing main slabs avoided: one main slab in signature; other bays are subordinate.
- Base vie too close avoided: base vie is south and outside fresh concrete.
- Truck access too narrow avoided: access lane is reserved on the south-east.
- Stage looking like terrassement avoided: no pit/dirt-pile/excavator narrative; focus is slab, rebar, formwork, pump, mixer.
